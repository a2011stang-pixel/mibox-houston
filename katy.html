Portable Storage in Katy, Texas

Serving Houston's most sought-after western suburb - home to top schools and Energy Corridor professionals.

Katy has evolved from a small railroad town into one of the Houston metro's most desirable communities, with a greater Katy area population exceeding 350,000 residents. Renowned for its nationally-ranked Katy ISD schools, safe neighborhoods, and family-friendly atmosphere, Katy attracts professionals from the nearby Energy Corridor and families seeking top-tier education. Master-planned communities like Cinco Ranch, Firethorne, Elyson, Cane Island, and Cross Creek Ranch offer resort-style amenities, while Katy Mills Mall and LaCenterra at Cinco Ranch provide world-class shopping.

Why Katy Residents Choose MI-BOX

  • Energy Corridor Relocations: Oil and gas professionals frequently relocate to Katy. Our portable storage provides flexible solutions for corporate moves, whether you're coming from across the country or across town.
  • New Construction Moves: With builders like Toll Brothers, David Weekley, and Taylor Morrison constantly adding homes in Elyson, Cane Island, and Jordan Ranch, new homeowners need temporary storage during the move-in process.
  • Home Staging & Sales: Katy's competitive real estate market means sellers need to declutter fast. Our containers help stage homes for quick sales without the hassle of off-site storage facilities.
  • Football Season Festivities: Katy is football country! Our containers are perfect for storing tailgate equipment, sports gear, and band equipment for Katy ISD's championship programs.
