Portable Storage in Richmond, Texas

Serving Fort Bend County's historic seat with flexible storage solutions along the Brazos River.

Richmond is the historic heart of Fort Bend County, established in 1837 and serving as the county seat ever since. With a population of approximately 13,000 in the city proper and over 100,000 in the greater Richmond area including Pecan Grove, this community blends Texas heritage with modern growth. The charming Historic Downtown along Morton Street features Victorian-era buildings, while new developments continue to expand along the Brazos River corridor and Highway 59/I-69.

Why Richmond Residents Choose MI-BOX

  • Historic Home Renovations: Richmond's older neighborhoods near downtown feature homes dating back to the early 1900s. Our portable containers provide on-site storage during restoration projects, protecting antiques and heirlooms.
  • Brazos River Flooding: Properties along the Brazos River face periodic flooding risks. MI-BOX containers offer quick-deploy storage for valuable items during flood watches and emergency evacuations.
  • Estate Sales & Transitions: Richmond's established families often need storage during estate settlements. Month-to-month flexibility lets you sort through generations of belongings at your own pace.
  • Pecan Grove Growth: The Pecan Grove Plantation community continues expanding with new construction. Our containers help families stage moves and store items during the building process.
