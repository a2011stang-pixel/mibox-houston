Portable Storage in Bellaire, Texas

Serving one of Houston's most prestigious residential enclaves with flexible storage solutions.

Bellaire is an affluent independent city of approximately 19,000 residents, completely surrounded by Houston. Known as the "City of Homes," Bellaire features beautiful tree-lined streets, excellent schools including the highly-rated Bellaire High School, and a strong sense of community. This established neighborhood attracts families who value quality education, walkable streets, and proximity to the Texas Medical Center, Galleria, and downtown Houston.

Why Bellaire Residents Choose MI-BOX

  • Major Home Renovations: Bellaire's older homes from the 1940s-1960s are prime candidates for extensive remodeling. Our containers protect furniture and belongings on-site during kitchen, bathroom, and whole-home renovation projects.
  • Estate Sales & Downsizing: Long-time Bellaire residents often have decades of accumulated belongings. Our month-to-month rentals give you time to sort through items, stage for estate sales, or prepare for downsizing.
  • Medical Center Professionals: With the Texas Medical Center nearby, many Bellaire residents are medical professionals who need flexible storage during relocations, fellowships, or extended travel.
  • Seasonal Storage: Bellaire's beautiful homes often host holiday gatherings and events. Store seasonal decorations, outdoor furniture, and party supplies conveniently.
