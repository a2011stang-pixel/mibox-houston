Portable Storage in Tomball, Texas

Serving a community that blends small-town charm with rapid growth along the 249 corridor.

Tomball is one of the Houston area's most dynamic communities, seamlessly blending its rich German heritage and historic downtown with explosive suburban growth. With a population approaching 15,000 in the city proper and over 100,000 in the greater Tomball area, this community along State Highway 249 has become one of Northwest Houston's most desirable destinations. Founded by German settlers in the 1800s, Tomball proudly maintains its heritage through events like the Tomball German Heritage Festival while welcoming new families to master-planned communities and excellent Tomball ISD schools.

Why Tomball Residents Choose MI-BOX

  • New Construction Everywhere: From Rosehill Reserve to Northgrove to Wildwood at Northpointe, new homes are going up constantly. MI-BOX helps new homeowners store belongings during the move-in and settling process.
  • Historic Home Renovations: Owners of charming older homes near downtown Tomball often need storage during restoration projects. Our containers protect antiques and heirlooms on-site.
  • Seasonal & Festival Storage: With the German Festival, Holiday Parade, and numerous community events, local businesses and organizations use our containers for event supplies and seasonal inventory.
  • Hurricane Preparedness: The Tomball area saw significant flooding during past storms. Residents now keep emergency supplies accessible with portable storage units.
  • Acreage Properties: Many Tomball-area residents have larger lots and rural properties. Our containers are perfect for farm equipment storage, workshop overflow, or organizing barns.
