Portable Storage in Friendswood, Texas

Serving one of Houston's most desirable family communities with flexible storage solutions.

Friendswood is a charming, upscale suburb of approximately 40,000 residents that consistently ranks among the best places to live in Texas. Uniquely split between Galveston and Harris counties, Friendswood is known for its excellent schools in both Friendswood ISD and Clear Creek ISD, tree-lined neighborhoods, and strong sense of community. Located near Clear Lake, NASA Johnson Space Center, and League City, residents enjoy easy access to both Houston and the coast.

Why Friendswood Residents Choose MI-BOX

  • Families Upgrading Homes: Friendswood's top-rated schools attract families ready to move up to larger homes. Our containers make the transition seamless while you prepare your current home for sale.
  • Empty Nesters Downsizing: Many long-time Friendswood residents are ready to simplify. Our month-to-month storage gives you time to sort through decades of memories at your own pace.
  • Home Renovations: Friendswood's established neighborhoods feature homes ready for updates. Keep furniture and belongings protected on-site during kitchen and bathroom remodels.
  • NASA Relocations: With proximity to Johnson Space Center, we regularly serve aerospace professionals moving to or from the area.
