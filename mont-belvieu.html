Portable Storage in Mont Belvieu, Texas

Serving the energy capital of the Gulf Coast with flexible storage solutions.

Mont Belvieu is a rapidly growing city of approximately 7,000 residents located east of Baytown, known worldwide as a critical hub for the energy industry. The city sits atop massive underground salt dome formations that provide storage for natural gas liquids, making it one of the most important energy infrastructure locations in North America. Companies like Enterprise Products, Targa Resources, and others operate major facilities here. Beyond the energy sector, Mont Belvieu offers a small-town atmosphere with excellent Barbers Hill ISD schools, the beautiful Eagle Pointe Golf Club, and easy access to I-10 for Houston commuters.

Why Mont Belvieu Residents Choose MI-BOX

  • Energy Industry Workers: With the area's energy facilities running 24/7, workers often relocate for projects or rotate on extended schedules. Our flexible month-to-month rentals work around unpredictable timelines.
  • New Construction: Mont Belvieu is booming with new home construction as families discover its excellent schools and rural feel. Keep your belongings safe during the building process or while moving into your new home.
  • Rural Property Storage: Many Mont Belvieu residents live on larger lots or acreage. Our containers are perfect for storing equipment, seasonal items, or belongings during property improvements.
  • Hurricane Preparedness: While Mont Belvieu is slightly more inland, hurricane prep remains important. Use our containers to protect valuables or stage emergency supplies.
