Portable Storage in Spring, Texas

Serving one of Houston's largest and most diverse unincorporated communities.

Spring is a sprawling, unincorporated community in northern Harris and southern Montgomery counties with a population exceeding 200,000 residents across its many neighborhoods. Anchored by the charming Old Town Spring shopping district and stretching from I-45 to the Grand Parkway (99), Spring offers everything from historic cottages to brand-new master-planned developments. The area is served by multiple school districts including Spring ISD, Klein ISD, and Conroe ISD, making it one of the most geographically diverse communities in Greater Houston.

Why Spring Residents Choose MI-BOX

  • Continuous New Development: With communities like Harmony, Gleannloch Farms, and Spring Trails constantly adding homes, new residents need flexible storage during their move-in process.
  • Home Renovations & Additions: Spring's mix of 1980s-era homes and newer construction means constant renovation activity. Our containers protect belongings on-site during kitchen remodels, room additions, and whole-home updates.
  • Old Town Spring Businesses: The 150+ shops and restaurants in Old Town Spring use portable storage for seasonal inventory, event supplies, and renovation projects.
  • Hurricane & Flood Preparedness: Parts of Spring saw significant flooding during Hurricane Harvey. Many residents now maintain emergency supplies in accessible portable storage.
  • Sports & Activities Storage: Spring families are active! Our containers help store sports equipment, camping gear, and seasonal items for busy households.
